How to install metamask? - Go to https://metamask.io/ website and click on download.

extension to chrome How to set up an account Once you have it downloaded, Click/Tap on Create a new wallet.

- Agree to improvement program (Optional), You can opt not to agree to these.

- Setup a strong password and Agree to terms and services.

- Next you will be shown a random 12-word secure phrase, Make sure to write it down or save it using a secure password manager.

This needs to be properly written and kept safe as the order of the words is also crucial.

- Click next and you will be redirected to next page where you have to select words according to your secret phrase (do it right and your account will be created)

How to get private key of your account?

Copy private key from highlighted area and paste it into Krypton connect wallet (add link of our connect page)
